Where the Money Goes

Loss #1: Unpaid VE Rework Time

Scenario: 15 projects/year, 2 VE cycles each, 3 hours per cycle

Annual compensation: $160,000 USD
Hourly value: $160,000 ÷ 1,920 hours = $83/hour
VE rework time: 15 projects × 2 cycles × 3 hours = 90 hours
Time lost: 90 hours × $83/hour
= $7,500 USD/year

Loss #2: Commission from Downgraded Scope

Scenario: Average $300K lighting package, 10% VE reduction, 5% commission

Average project scope: $300,000
VE scope reduction: 10% = $30,000
Commission rate: 5% (conservative)
Lost commission per project: $30,000 × 5% = $1,500
Annual loss: $1,500 × 15 projects
= $22,500 USD/year

Total Annual Impact
